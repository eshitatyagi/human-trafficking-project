🔬 Dataset Creation

Data Collection

Reviewed 2000+ news articles from verified sources (2014-2019)

Quality Control

Used Alexa.com rankings ≤100 for source reliability verification

Final Dataset

302 authentic cases across 10 key dimensions

Secondary Data

CTDC dataset: 90k+ cases (2004-2018) with extensive cleaning

🌍 Geographic Patterns

  • USA & Malaysia show highest female victim rates
  • USA appears as both source and destination hotspot
  • Thailand & Indonesia are primary destination countries
  • India shows high trafficking volume to other countries
  • Italy sting operation rescued ~1200 victims

👥 Demographics & Categories

  • Women (≥18 years) are most frequent victims
  • Age 9-17 most targeted among children
  • Ages 30-38 highly affected (labor trafficking)
  • Sexual Exploitation & Labor: 46.9% of cases
  • 59.4% of victims knew their trafficker

🎯 Key Dataset Features (10 Dimensions)

Source & Location

URL, Origin country, Destination country, City of crime

Victim Demographics

Number of people, Ages, Male/Female victim counts

Crime Classification

Category/type of trafficking, Date of occurrence

Temporal Analysis

Year-over-year trends showing increasing crime rates
